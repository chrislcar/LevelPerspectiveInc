# P1.1 — Current-State Diagnosis

The first of five catalog playbooks in Journey 1 (Strategic Foundation). Produces the diagnostic substrate from which future-state design proceeds in P1.2 onward.

## Scope

Methodology Steps 1–5 of the 11-step current-to-future-state methodology.

## Owns

- Slide 1 — Current-state Strategic Guidance Framework (SGF) with four elements: Center, Strategic Direction, Brand Image, Organizational Culture
- Slide 2 — Current-state Business Model Canvas (BMC) at postcard altitude with named pattern altitude
- Slide 3 — SWOT synthesis with cross-quadrant patterns
- Appendix 16 — Performance Assessment + Trend Assessment scorecards

## Does not own

Anything future-state, the three leadership decisions (P1.2), the QFL instrument (P1.2), shift selection (P1.2), pattern stacks (P1.3), KPIs (P1.4), roadmaps (P1.4), or the closing SGF bookend (P1.5).
