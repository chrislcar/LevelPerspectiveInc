## Doctrine vs spec

A **doctrine** statement is a principle that applies across multiple playbooks. A **spec** describes a specific artifact or step. If you find yourself writing the same principle into multiple specs, it belongs here as doctrine and gets referenced from the specs.

## Open Brain mirror

`open-brain-export.md` is generated by the Friday close ritual. The exported content is read-only in the repo; primary captures still happen in Open Brain. The mirror exists for continuity (Lens 6 recovery scenario) and for offline reference.
